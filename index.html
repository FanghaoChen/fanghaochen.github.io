01 · About关于

Roots & Routes根脉与航路

I study how Chinese networks quietly move goods, capital, technology, and ideas across borders — through the tangible vessels of migration: clan genealogies, migration archives, diaspora communities, and Chinese surnames. 我借助移民网络的具象实体——宗族族谱、移民档案、侨乡社群、华裔姓氏——研究华人网络如何在国与国之间悄然传输货物、资本、技术与观念。

02 · Research研究

Selected Works研究成果

My research focuses on the economics of ethnic networks — particularly overseas Chinese networks as intermediaries in trade, capital, technology, and knowledge flows between China and the global economy. I study their historical formation, long-run persistence, and evolving roles in China's industrial upgrading and firms' international expansion. 我的研究方向为族群网络经济学，重点关注海外华人网络在贸易、资本、技术与知识流动中连接中国与全球市场的中介作用，包括其历史形成、长期演化，以及在地缘政治变动下对中国产业升级与企业国际化的影响。

Chinese Character–English Spelling Two-way Crosswalk for Chinese Surnames华人姓氏中英文双向对照数据库

A probabilistic two-way crosswalk between Chinese surname characters and their English spellings across transliteration systems and dialect groups — infrastructure for cross-language record linkage in Chinese migration and diaspora studies.提供华人姓氏汉字与多种拼写体系及方言音译之间的英文双向概率对照，为华人移民研究中的跨语言数据匹配提供基础工具。

Chen, Xiong & Zhang. Journal of International Economics, 162, 104293.

Transnational Genealogies Database涉侨族谱地理分布数据

City–surname distribution of transnational genealogies held by the Jinan University Library — the historical geographic footprint of overseas-Chinese clan networks, for research on diaspora networks, migration history, and their long-run economic effects.记录暨南大学图书馆馆藏涉侨族谱在城市—姓氏层面的分布，刻画海外华人宗族网络的历史地理特征，可用于华人网络、移民史及其长期经济效应研究。
